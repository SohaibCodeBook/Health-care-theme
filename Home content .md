Know exactly where your every dollar stands. Our transparent billing platform gives you real time visibility into the health of your revenue cycle, so nothing is a guessing game. ### **Medical Credentialing Services**

Falling out of network or behind on credentials is one of the fastest ways to lose revenue. We manage the full lifecycle of provider enrollment and payer contracting, including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial networks like BCBS, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Our proprietary AI monitoring tracks open applications, renewals, and CAQH profiles in real time and flags stalled submissions before they cost you billable weeks. We also run quarterly benchmark audits to see how your fee schedules compare to regional market rates.

Our virtual staffing service gives your practice a trained, dependable professional backed by built in AI tools that surface priority tasks and organize follow up queues. A Med VA can take over call handling, scheduling, patient reactivation, intake coordination, verification of benefits, and prior authorization tracking, so your in person front desk can focus on the patients standing in front of them.
